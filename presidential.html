Selected DAX Measures

Total Votes

Total Votes = SUM(Votes[Votes])

Total Candidates

Total Candidates = COUNT(Candidates[Candidate_id])

Total Votes per County

Total Votes per County =
CALCULATE([Total Votes], ALLEXCEPT(Votes, County[County_id]))

Candidate Rank

Candidate Rank =
RANKX(ALL(Candidates[Candidate_id]), [Total Votes],, DESC, Dense)

These measures use common DAX patterns to power interactive reporting and ranking logic.
